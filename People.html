Out and Ally Lists

Visibility is important! LGBTQ+ mathematicians are everywhere, and we hope this list can serve as support for mathematicians at various places on their own LGBTQ+ journies and also as a resource for people looking to learn more about the climate at various universities.
For each mathematician, any identity words and pronouns listed were given by the respondent.

You can sign up to be on the outlist or the allylist or update your listing by filling out this form.
