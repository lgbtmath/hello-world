Student Chapters

Are you interested in having a Spectra chapter at your institution? Great! We are happy to work with you at setting this up and to celebrate your events and work. Setting things up is easy:

  1. Set-up some by-laws (we recommend following AWM's Student Chapter example while we work on Spectra specific ones)
  2. Choose initial officers
  3. Email lgbtmath@gmail.com with this information!

Once everything is set up, we will include information about chapter here.
